I'm a cake maker based in Thatcham, West Berkshire. I started baking and decorating cakes as a hobby from a young age with the dream that I could turn my passion into a career. As I only produce cakes part time, I am unable to take a lot of orders so if you are interested in me making a cake for your celebration please contact me as early as possible.
